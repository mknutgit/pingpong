Ping & Pong

Step on up and play!

Rules:

  1. Numbers divisable by 3 become 'ping'
  2. Numbers divisable by 5 become 'pong'
  3. Numbers divisable by 15 become 'pingpong'

How to play:

Enter a number and select 'see the result'. Selcect the 'play again' button to play as many times as you would like.
